ðŸ”¹ Switch Statement

The switch statement checks different cases.

let day = "Monday";
switch (day) {
  case "Monday":
    console.log("Start of the week!");
    break;
  case "Friday":
    console.log("Weekend is near!");
    break;
  default:
    console.log("Just another day.");
}
